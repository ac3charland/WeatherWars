Weather Wars

Instructions

This is a turn based fighting game that involves two players selecting one of three choices of cities to fight with.

To begin the game, select a city. City health is determined by factors including safety, overall happiness and overcast intensity. Each city has 4 different possible attacks, each attack is different depending on the current weather of the selected city. The four attacks are; basic. hot, cold and storm. the basic attack deals a base damage of 10 with no other bonuses or detriments, hot, which deals 17.5 damage to the enemy player while, damaging the attacking player for 5 damage. Cold, which deals 15 damage to the enemy however you miss your next attaking turn, and finally Storm, which deals 5 damage while enabling you to attack again immediately.

The game ends when either players health reaches zero. Good luck, have fun.
